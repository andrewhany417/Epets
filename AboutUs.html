About E-Pet

Who Are We?

We are but four people who came together, pondering over the means of making pets'living standards better. We started as a small care group but evolved into one with good standard connections in anything pet-related. Shelters, community of volunteers willing to pet-sit for others, vets, you name it!

Truth is, while where we started is important, where we are headed matters more at the time being. We wish to make homes and pets fill with glee and warmth. Share the fun with us!

We provide our contacts - vets and the like - to our community of lovely users. We want not only to cater to your pets' health needs but also to spoil these little babies. Things like toys and accessories and grooming shops or products available in your area can also be listed for you at our site. We'd like nothing more than for you and your overly dressed pet to stand out.

Our Mission

  • Make sure animals are well taken care of.
  • Make sure homes are full of joy when it comes to our services.
  • Increase the population of our community.
  • Facilitate means of taking care of pets.
  • Raise awareness on many care tips people may be unaware of.
  • Create a safe and home-y community of pet-owners.
  • Provide a safe place for your - our - pets.
