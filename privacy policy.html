Privacy Policies

1. We care about our pets.

  • We send out notice to each previous pet owner that has put their pets up for adoption.
  • We regulary inspect our sister shelters.
  • We hold surprise interviews and housevisits to our volunteers.
  • We have robust means of communication.

2. We care about your health.

  • Our pets' dietary and medicinal needs are reviewed once they come into our care and we proceed to make this information readily available.
  • Vets make sure no form of contagion is affecting our pets nor our pet owners or volunteers.
  • Regular visits to vets are always recommended, especially during modern times where viruses are spreading. E-Pets provides a direct means of contact with nearby recommended veterenarians who are more that happy to help.

3. We care about your trust and comfort.

  • Any admitted discomfort on our users' side will be addressed immediately and is of the utmost importance to our community of workers.
  • Criticism is welcome to us; we take our feedback very seriously.
  • Home is what we want to feel like to both the pets and people.
  • Any information you are to be asked to share shall remain as private as you wish it to be.

4. You are our priority.

  • Your feedback comes first; contacting us through any of our social media platforms or using the contact form will be addressed as soon as possible.
  • If any trouble is being faced when two people are communicating through our website, a worker can be asked to interfere in favor of both parties.
  • First visits to pets are automatically made free after you have adopted through our site, for every pet. You took them in, we make sure they stay that way for a long time!

Reminder:

We continuiusly look out for every member of our community, regardless of what their role is.
